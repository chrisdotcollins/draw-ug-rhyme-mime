Instructions

Choose 4 words from the 8 given, then get your partner to guess those four words by doing one of the following:

  • DRAW one word! Quickly quickly now, but no writing letters!

  • UG one word! You are a caveman, so speak like one! You only know one-syllable words and you can't use THE, AND, or any numbers!

  • RHYME one word! Can you create three clues with three answers that rhyme? One of those answers must be your word:
    Where I put rubbish, where baked beans live, when we score more goals...
    BIN, TIN, WIN!!!

  • MIME one word! Can you play charades? You have to mime the final word!

You have 90 seconds, so chop chop! If you can't guess, anyone on the team can shout SKIP! and move on to the next word. You get 2 points per word you guess and 2 bonus points if you get all four! But, if you skip or can't get any, the other team can get a bonus point if they can answer!
